bio: Saburō Kōga worked at the Ministry of Agriculture and Commerce where he met Edogawa Rampo and Udaru Ōshita. While working at the ministry, Saburō Kōga started writing detective novels. In 1923, his detective novel 真珠塔の秘密 is accepted in the magazine 新青年, and Saburō Kōga choses his pen name. His debut novel  follows Edogawa Rampo's own debut 二銭銅貨 by only 4 months.
