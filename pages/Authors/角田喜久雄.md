bio: Kikuo Tsunoda has written Chuanqi, social novels and detective novels. Among the detective novels, the 加賀美捜査一課長 series (Kagami, chief of the First Criminal Investigation Division) and 明石良輔記者 series (journalist Ryosuke Akashi) both include novels and short stories. The first novel of the Kagami series was a nominee for the first Mystery Writers of Japan Award (the year Seishi Yokomizo won with The Honjin Murders)
