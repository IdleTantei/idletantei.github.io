Japanese authors of detective novels

江戸川 乱歩 - Edogawa Rampo (1894-1965)

Edogawa Rampo is the father of fictional detective Kogoro Akechi. The Kogoro Akechi series is followed by the Boys Detective Club, a series of detective novels for young reader featuring a group of teens mentored by Kogoro Akechi. Edogawa Rampo has also written numerous stnand alone mystery/horror stories, most of which are translated into English.
